StudentPlus FAQ

Find answers to frequently asked questions about StudentPlus.

Account Access

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Click the "Forgot password?" on the login page. You'll be prompted to enter your school email address, and reset instructions will be sent to that email.

I am having trouble logging in with my credentials. What should I do?
  • Ensure you're using the correct username and password.
  • Check if Caps Lock is enabled.
  • Try cleaning your browser cache and cookies.
  • Contact your school IT administration if issues persist.
How do I create a new account?

Account creation is managed by your school administration. Please contact them for access to the platform.

Can I use StudentPlus on my mobile device?

Yes, StudentPlus is optimised for both desktop and mobile use. Simply visit the login page from your mobile browser.

Technical Issues

The login page isn't working properly. What should I do?
  • Try refreshing the page.
  • Clear your browser cache.
  • Try a different browser.
  • Check your internet connection.
  • If none of the above solved the issue, contact technical support for further assistance.
I'm experiencing slow loading times. How can I fix this?
  • Check your internet connection.
  • Close unnecessary browser tabs and applications.
  • Try using a different browser.
Who should I contact if I encounter technical issues?
  • Click the "Need help?" link at the bottom of the login page to connect with technical support.
  • You can also contact your school's IT administration for assistance.
