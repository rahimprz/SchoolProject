Teacher Dashboard FAQs

Find answers to frequently asked questions about using the StudentPlus dashboard.

General Usage

How do I navigate between different sections of the dashboard?

Use the navigation links at the top of the page to switch between Seating Plan Dashboard, Behavior Analytics, and Notifications.

How do I view data for a specific class?

Use the "Switch Class" dropdown in the subheader to select the class you want to view.

Can I customise what information is displayed on my graphs?

Yes, use the Advanced Filter section on the Behavior Analytics page to customise the data displayed. You can filter by class, subject, student, behavior type, date range, etc.

Seating Plan Management

How do I create a new seating plan?

Select your class from the dropdown, then drag and drop students from the "Unassigned Students" section to the desired seats. Then save the plan when you are finished editing it.

Can I rearrange students' seats?

Yes, simply drag and drop students to different seats within the seating plan.

How do I randomise the seating arrangements?

Click the "Randomise Seating" button at the top of the seating plan page.

Can I modify the number of seats in my classroom?

Yes, modifying the classroom layout (adding or removing seats) requires assistance from technical support. We recommend finalising your classroom layout before the term begins to avoid disruptions. Please contact support if you need changes to your layout.

What do different colours on student cards represent?
  • Green: Students in the top 25% by behavior points.
  • Orange: Students in the middle 50% by behavior points.
  • Red: Students in the bottom 25% by behavior points.
Can I customise the behavior category names?

Yes, in the Settings page under Behavior Management you can customise the category names based on your class preferences and student age groups. Options include colours, animal themes, celebrities or fully custom names of your choice.

Recording Student Behavior

How do I record positive behavior for a student?
  • Click the "Award" button on the student's card.
  • Select the number of points and the reason for the award.
  • Click "Submit".
How do I record behavior concerns?
  • Click the "Deduct" button on student's card.
  • Select the number of points to deduct and the reason.
  • Click "Submit".
Can I add custom reasons for behavior points?

Yes, select "Other" from the reason dropdown and enter your reason in the field that appears.

How can I view a student's information?

Click "..." button on a student's card to view student information.

Data Analysis and Reports

How do I generate reports on student behavior?
  • Navigate to the Behavior Analytics section.
  • Use the filter to select the relevant data.
  • Click the "Print Report" button.
What report formats are available?
  • PDF document
  • Excel Spreadsheet
  • Direct Print
How do I filter behavior data?
  • Use the Advanced Filters section to customise your view.
  • You can filter by class, subject, student, behavior type, data range, etc.
  • Click "Apply Filters" to update the view.

Settings

How do I update my personal information?
  • Navigate to Settings, then Account Settings.
  • Update your information in the relevant fields.
  • Click "Save Changes".
How do I change my password?
  • Go to Setting then Password & Security.
  • Enter your current password and your new password.
  • Click "Update Password".
How do I manage my active sessions?
  • Go to Settings then Password & Security.
  • Scroll down to the Active Sessions section.
  • Click "Sign Out" next to any session you want to terminate.
How do I edit behaviour categories?
  • Go to settings then Behavior Management.
  • Edit existing categories, edit or delete.
  • Click "Save Changes" when finished.
Can I set different behavior categories for different classes?

Yes, use the "Apply to Class" dropdown to select which class the categories should apply to.

How do I reset student behavior points?
  • You can enable automatic point resets at the end of each term by toggling "Auto-reset Points".
  • To manually reset points, use the "Reset to Default" button.
Can students see their behavior categories?

You can control that with the "Show Behavior Categories to Students" toggle.

How do I enable dark mode?
  • Go to Settings then Appearance Settings.
  • Toggle the "Dark Mode" switch.
How can I adjust the font size?
  • Go to Settings then Appearance Settings.
  • Select your preferred size from the "Front Size" dropdown.
Are there accessibility options available?
  • Yes, go to Settings then Appearance Settings then Accessibility Settings.
  • Options include High Contrast Mode, Screen Reader Support, and Reduced Motion.
